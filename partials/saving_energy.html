Super Energy Saving

5 Tick Energy Label is the highest energy-efficiency rating for inverter type air conditioners. The energy labeling was introduced by Singapore's Ministry for the Environment and Water Resources. This allows households to select more energy-efficienct models andso reduce their electricity bills.
