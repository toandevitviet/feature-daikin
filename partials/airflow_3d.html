3D Airflow

Vertical Auto-Swing automatically moves the flaps up and down and Horizontal Auto-Swing automatically moves the louvres left and right. With the combination of both, you enjoy a 3D airflow where air is circulated to every part of the room for uniform cooling of even large spaces.
