Intelligent Eye

Intelligent Eye prevents energy wastage by using its infrared sensor to detect human movement in a room. When there is no movement, Intelligent Eye increases the temperature by 2oC to give energy savings of up to 20%. This reduces energy wastage if, for example, you forget to turn off the air conditioner. The function can be conveniently activated from the remote controller.
