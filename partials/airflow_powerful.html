Powerful Airflow

Power-Airflow Dual Flaps can flatten out during cooling operation to deliver cool air to the farthest corners of a room. Together with the smoothly curved Wide-Angle Louvers, you enjoy wide airflow coverage for effective cooling operation no matter where the indoor unit is placed in a room.
